How BCBS Coverage Works at Lumen

Lumen is an in-network provider with BlueCross BlueShield, the largest health insurance network in the United States. With over 115 million members across all 50 states, BCBS is the most common insurance we see — and we accept virtually all BCBS plans for telehealth psychiatry.

Thanks to the BlueCard program, your BCBS plan works at Lumen regardless of which local Blue plan issued it. Whether you have Anthem Blue Cross in California, CareFirst BCBS in Maryland, Empire BCBS in New York, Highmark BCBS in Pennsylvania, or any other local Blue plan, your coverage travels with you through our telehealth platform.

BlueCard program

The BCBS BlueCard program allows members of any local Blue plan to receive in-network benefits from providers contracted with a different local Blue plan. This means your BCBS card works at Lumen no matter where it was issued.

What Your BCBS Plan Typically Covers

BlueCross BlueShield plans are required to provide mental health parity, meaning your psychiatric benefits are comparable to your medical and surgical benefits. Here is what most BCBS plans cover:

  • Initial psychiatric evaluation: Covered as a specialist visit with your standard specialist copay.
  • Medication management: Follow-up visits covered at in-network rates, typically 20-30 minutes.
  • Telehealth visits: BCBS covers telehealth psychiatry at in-network rates. No separate facility fee.
  • Prescription medications: Covered through your BCBS pharmacy benefit. Formulary varies by local plan.
