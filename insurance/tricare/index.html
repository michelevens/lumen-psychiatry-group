How Tricare Coverage Works at Lumen

Lumen is an authorized Tricare provider for telehealth psychiatric services. We accept Tricare Prime, Tricare Select, and Tricare Reserve Select, serving active duty service members, their dependents, National Guard and Reserve members, retirees, and military families across all Tricare regions.

Mental health is a critical priority for the military community. Studies show that approximately 1 in 4 active duty service members experience a mental health condition, yet stigma and access barriers prevent many from seeking care. Our telehealth platform provides a private, confidential way to access psychiatric care without visiting a military treatment facility or civilian office — reducing both stigma and logistical barriers.

Tricare Plans We Accept

  • Tricare Prime: $0 copay for active duty. Dependents may have small copays. Referral from PCM typically required.
  • Tricare Select: Cost-sharing after annual deductible. No referral required for network providers.
  • Tricare Reserve Select: Cost-sharing similar to Select for National Guard and Reserve members.
  • Tricare for Life: For military retirees with Medicare. Medicare pays first, Tricare covers remaining costs.
Confidential care for service members

Telehealth psychiatry through Lumen is conducted outside the military health system. While we coordinate with your care team when requested, seeking mental health care through a civilian provider can provide an additional layer of privacy for active duty members concerned about career implications.

What Your Tricare Plan Typically Covers

  • Active duty (Prime): $0 copay for behavioral health visits. Referral from PCM required.
  • Dependents (Prime): Small copay per visit after point-of-service deductible if applicable.
  • Tricare Select: After annual deductible ($150 individual / $300 family for E-5+), Tricare pays 80% of allowed charges.
  • Telehealth visits: Covered at the same rate as in-person across all Tricare plans.
