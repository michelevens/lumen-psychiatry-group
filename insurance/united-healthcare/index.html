How UnitedHealthcare Coverage Works at Lumen

Lumen is an in-network provider with UnitedHealthcare (UHC), the largest single health insurance carrier in the United States with over 50 million members. Whether you have a UHC plan through your employer, the marketplace, or a subsidiary like Oxford Health Plans or UMR, your telehealth psychiatry visits are covered at in-network rates.

UHC's behavioral health benefits are managed through Optum Behavioral Health, one of the largest behavioral health organizations in the country. As an Optum-credentialed provider, Lumen handles all claims and authorizations through the Optum system, so you get seamless coverage for psychiatric services.

UHC Plans We Accept

  • UnitedHealthcare commercial — employer-sponsored HMO, PPO, EPO, and POS plans
  • Oxford Health Plans — Liberty and Freedom plans (NY, NJ, CT area)
  • UMR — self-funded employer plans administered by UHC
  • UHC marketplace plans — individual and family ACA plans
  • Optum behavioral health — any plan with Optum managing mental health benefits
Oxford Health Plans members

If you have Oxford Liberty or Oxford Freedom through your employer in the tri-state area, Lumen accepts your plan at in-network rates. Oxford plans provide the same Optum behavioral health network as standard UHC plans.

What Your UHC Plan Typically Covers

  • Initial psychiatric evaluation: Covered as a specialist visit with your standard UHC specialist copay.
  • Medication management: Follow-up visits covered at in-network rates, typically 20-30 minutes.
  • Telehealth visits: UHC covers virtual psychiatry at in-network rates with no additional facility fee.
  • Prescription medications: Covered through your UHC/OptumRx pharmacy benefit.
