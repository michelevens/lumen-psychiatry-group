How Aetna Coverage Works at Lumen

Lumen is an in-network provider with Aetna, which means your telehealth psychiatry visits are covered at your plan's in-network rate. Whether you have an Aetna plan through your employer, the Health Insurance Marketplace, or Aetna Better Health (Medicaid), we can see you for psychiatric care with minimal out-of-pocket costs.

As an Aetna member, you are entitled to coverage for outpatient mental health services, including psychiatric evaluations and medication management. Aetna CVS Health plans, which now encompass most Aetna products following the CVS Health merger, provide robust behavioral health benefits that cover telehealth at the same rate as in-person visits.

Understanding Your Aetna Benefits

  • Copay: Most Aetna plans have a specialist copay of $20-$50 per psychiatric visit
  • Deductible: Some plans require you to meet your deductible before copay-only pricing kicks in
  • Coinsurance: After deductible, many Aetna PPO plans cover 80-90% of the allowed amount
  • Out-of-pocket max: Once reached, Aetna covers 100% of in-network services
Free benefits verification

We verify your Aetna benefits before your first appointment at no charge. You will know your exact copay, deductible status, and any authorization requirements before your visit.

What Your Aetna Plan Typically Covers

Aetna provides mental health parity, meaning your behavioral health benefits must be comparable to your medical/surgical benefits. Here is what most Aetna plans cover for psychiatric care:

  • Initial psychiatric evaluation: Covered as a specialist visit. Your first appointment is a comprehensive 60-minute assessment.
  • Medication management: Follow-up visits for ongoing prescribing and dose adjustments, typically 20-30 minutes.
  • Telehealth visits: Aetna covers telehealth at in-network rates with no facility fee. You pay the same copay as an in-person visit.
  • Prescription medications: Covered through your Aetna pharmacy benefit (separate from visit copay). Aetna CVS Health members may get additional pharmacy savings at CVS locations.
