How Ambetter Coverage Works at Lumen

Lumen is an in-network provider with Ambetter, the Health Insurance Marketplace brand of Centene Corporation. Ambetter is one of the largest ACA marketplace insurers in the country, serving millions of individuals and families who purchase coverage through Healthcare.gov and state exchanges.

If you bought your health insurance through the ACA marketplace and selected an Ambetter plan, your telehealth psychiatry visits at Lumen are covered at in-network rates. This includes members who receive premium tax credits or cost-sharing reductions — your subsidies apply to mental health services just like any other covered care.

Understanding Your Ambetter Benefits

  • Essential health benefits: All ACA plans must cover mental health services, including psychiatry
  • Plan tiers: Ambetter offers Bronze, Silver, Gold, and sometimes Platinum tiers with different copays
  • Subsidies apply: Premium tax credits and cost-sharing reductions lower your out-of-pocket costs
  • Telehealth covered: Ambetter covers telehealth visits at in-network rates
ACA marketplace mental health coverage

Under the Affordable Care Act, mental health and substance use disorder services are one of the 10 essential health benefits that all marketplace plans must cover. Your Ambetter plan covers psychiatric evaluations, medication management, and telehealth visits as essential covered services.

What Your Ambetter Plan Typically Covers

  • Initial psychiatric evaluation: Covered as a specialist visit. Bronze plans may apply deductible first.
  • Medication management: Follow-up visits at in-network rates, typically 20-30 minutes.
  • Telehealth visits: Covered at in-network rates with no additional facility fee.
  • Prescription medications: Covered through Ambetter's pharmacy benefit with tiered formulary.
