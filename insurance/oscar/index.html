How Oscar Health Coverage Works at Lumen

Lumen is an in-network provider with Oscar Health, the technology-driven health insurance company that has redefined the insurance experience for millions of marketplace members. Oscar is especially popular among individuals and families who purchased coverage through the ACA Health Insurance Marketplace, and their user-friendly app makes managing your mental health benefits easier than ever.

Oscar Health was built with a technology-first philosophy — and so was Lumen. Our telehealth psychiatry platform pairs naturally with Oscar's digital-forward approach to healthcare. You can track your benefits, view claims, and manage your costs through the Oscar app, while receiving board-certified psychiatric care through our secure video platform.

Understanding Your Oscar Benefits

  • Copay: Oscar specialist copays typically range from $25-$50 depending on your plan tier (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum)
  • Deductible: Bronze and Silver plans may require you to meet your deductible before copays apply
  • Cost transparency: Oscar's app shows your estimated cost before each visit
  • Telehealth friendly: Oscar covers telehealth at the same rate as in-person visits
Oscar's tech-forward approach

Oscar members can use their app to view their behavioral health benefits, check their deductible status, and see real-time claims from Lumen visits. Oscar's concierge team can also help you understand your mental health coverage if you have questions.

What Your Oscar Plan Typically Covers

  • Initial psychiatric evaluation: Covered as a specialist visit. Your first appointment is a comprehensive 60-minute assessment.
  • Medication management: Follow-up visits at in-network rates, typically 20-30 minutes.
  • Telehealth visits: Oscar covers telehealth at in-network rates — no extra fees for virtual visits.
  • Prescription medications: Covered through Oscar's pharmacy benefit with tiered formulary pricing.
