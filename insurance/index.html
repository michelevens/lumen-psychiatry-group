What Insurance Typically Covers

Most insurance plans cover psychiatric services when provided by an in-network, board-certified provider. At Lumen, the following services are typically covered by your plan:

  • Psychiatric evaluations — comprehensive 60-minute initial assessments
  • Medication management — ongoing follow-up visits for prescribing and dose adjustments
  • Telehealth visits — all major carriers now cover telehealth at the same rate as in-person
  • Diagnostic assessments — ADHD screening, depression scales, anxiety evaluations

Your specific coverage depends on your plan type (HMO, PPO, EPO), deductible status, and whether you have met your out-of-pocket maximum. We verify all of this before your first visit.
