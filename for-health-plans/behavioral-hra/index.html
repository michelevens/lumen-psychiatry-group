The Gap

Behavioral measures your HRA program is probably missing.

General-purpose HRA vendors are optimized for HCC capture and physical-health screening. Behavioral health HEDIS measures require a different clinical workflow — and they're the gaps plans lose Stars points on.

HEDIS DSF

Depression Screening & Follow-Up

PHQ-9 administered, scored, and clinically interpreted — with a documented follow-up plan when positive. Not just a checkbox on an intake form.

HEDIS SUD

Substance Use Disorder

SBIRT-level alcohol and drug screening (G0442/G0443 eligible), with brief intervention and referral when indicated — documented to payer specifications.

Stars / CAHPS

Member experience on BH topics

Members report higher satisfaction when BH concerns are addressed by a specialist, not a general-assessment clinician. Drives CAHPS "Getting Needed Care" scores.

Our Model

Built on a mental-health specialty practice — not a generalist HRA operation.

Lumen Psychiatry Group operates a telehealth psychiatric practice across 20+ states. The same NPs, infrastructure, and documentation workflows used for longitudinal psychiatric care deliver the behavioral HRA visit.

What's different

  • Psychiatric NPs, not generalist NPs. Every visit is conducted by a clinician whose full-time practice is behavioral health.
  • Telehealth-first. No travel cost, no scheduling friction. Visits completed in days, not weeks.
  • Full-practice-state NP scope. No physician co-signature requirement in our core states.
  • NCQA-compliant HRA templates with BH measures built in — not bolted on.

What we don't do

  • We don't replace your primary HRA vendor. Signify, Matrix, and HarmonyCares do in-home physical assessment well.
  • We don't chase HCC volume. Our value is measured in BH HEDIS closure rate and Stars impact, not HCCs per visit.
  • We don't treat acute issues on the visit. We screen, document, and refer — matching AWV/HRA scope.
  • We don't lock members in. If longitudinal psych care is needed, we refer to the member's in-network provider.
How We Integrate

Two deployment options depending on your existing program.

Most plans start with the add-on model because it requires no changes to their primary HRA contract. Standalone works best for regional MA plans without an in-home vendor in place.

Option A

Add-on to your existing HRA vendor

Your in-home vendor (Signify, Matrix, HarmonyCares) completes the physical assessment. We follow with a 30-minute telehealth behavioral visit that closes PHQ-9, GAD-7, SUD, and related HEDIS measures.

Fastest to contract · no disruption to primary vendor
Option B

Standalone behavioral HRA

For members where a full in-home HRA isn't indicated or cost-effective, we deliver a focused behavioral HRA visit covering BH HEDIS measures plus AWV-eligible elements within NP scope.

Best for regional plans · dual-eligible panels · high-BH-burden cohorts
