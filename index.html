Continuity of Care. Every Visit.

Lumen Psychiatry Group is a telehealth psychiatric practice built on one principle that large platforms have abandoned: you see the same provider every time. No rotating clinicians, no retelling your story, no starting over.

Our board-certified providers deliver comprehensive psychiatric evaluations, medication management, and therapy across 31 states via secure video. We accept most major insurance plans and offer flat-rate Direct Psychiatry Care memberships for patients who prefer transparent, subscription-based pricing.

We also provide on-site psychiatric services for skilled nursing and assisted living facilities in the Austin, TX area and ER tele-psych consultations in all states where we are licensed — extending our care beyond the screen to where it's needed most.

Frequently Asked Questions

You'll connect with your provider via secure HD video from any device — phone, tablet, or computer. Sessions are conducted through our HIPAA-compliant platform, just like an in-person visit. Your provider can evaluate symptoms, provide therapy, prescribe medications, and coordinate your care plan.

We accept most major insurance plans including Aetna, BCBS, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, Humana, Oscar, Ambetter, Medicare, Tricare, and Molina. We verify your benefits before your first visit at no charge. If you're out-of-network, we provide superbills for reimbursement.

DPC is a subscription-based model where you pay a flat monthly fee for access to mental health services — no insurance needed. Plans start at $99/month and include visits, medication management, unlimited messaging with your provider, and priority scheduling. It's ideal for those without insurance, with high deductibles, or who prefer a simpler payment model.

Yes. Our board-certified psychiatric providers can prescribe most psychiatric medications via telehealth, including antidepressants, anti-anxiety medications, mood stabilizers, and ADHD medications. Controlled substances are prescribed in compliance with state and federal telehealth prescribing regulations. Prescriptions are sent electronically to your preferred pharmacy.

Most new patients can be seen within 3-5 business days. DPC subscribers get priority scheduling with next-day availability in most cases. For urgent concerns, we offer same-day crisis support slots. We also maintain a cancellation waitlist for earlier openings.
