Psychiatric Care Rooted in Florida

As a Florida-based practice, Lumen Psychiatry Group brings deep local expertise to mental health care across the Sunshine State. Our physical office in Austin serves the greater Orlando metro area, while our telehealth platform extends board-certified psychiatric services to patients in every corner of Florida — from Jacksonville to Miami, Tampa to Tallahassee.

Florida's warm climate and laid-back reputation can mask a serious mental health crisis. The state ranks among the lowest in the nation for per-capita mental health funding, and millions of Floridians live in areas designated as mental health professional shortage areas. Lumen was founded to bridge that gap — providing timely, expert psychiatric care without the 2-3 month wait times common at traditional practices.

In-Person + Telehealth

Unlike most telehealth-only practices, Lumen also provides on-site psychiatric services at nursing facilities, assisted living communities, and behavioral health centers throughout the Austin, TX area — plus ER tele-psychiatry consultations for hospital emergency departments.
