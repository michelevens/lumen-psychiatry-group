Our Provider

Dr. Priya Patel, DNP, PMHNP-BC, FNP-BC is a board-certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP) and Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP) with extensive training in diagnosing and treating psychiatric conditions across the lifespan.

Dr. Michel holds a Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP) degree and dual board certifications from the American Nurses Credentialing Center (ANCC). With expertise spanning psychiatric evaluation, medication management, psychotherapy, and pharmacogenomics, Dr. Michel provides comprehensive, individualized care for adults experiencing mental health challenges.

As the founder of Lumen Psychiatry Group, Dr. Michel built the practice around a core belief: everyone deserves access to high-quality psychiatric care, regardless of where they live. Through secure telehealth technology, Lumen extends that care to patients in more than 30 states.

Our Approach

Lumen takes an evidence-based, patient-centered approach to psychiatric care. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all treatment. Every patient receives a thorough evaluation, an individualized treatment plan, and ongoing support as their needs evolve.

  • Same provider, every visit. You see the same board-certified provider at every appointment. No rotating clinicians, no retelling your story.
  • Evidence-based treatment. Every recommendation is grounded in current research and clinical guidelines. We use standardized screening tools, validated rating scales, and pharmacogenomic testing when appropriate.
  • Comprehensive evaluations. Initial appointments are 60 minutes — long enough to understand your full clinical picture before making treatment decisions.
  • Accessible care. Telehealth in 30+ states means your care goes where you go. In-network with 10 major insurance plans, plus flat-rate DPC plans for those without insurance.
  • Collaborative approach. We work alongside your therapist, primary care provider, or other specialists to ensure coordinated care.

Conditions We Treat

  • ADHD — Evaluation, behavioral strategies, and medication management
  • Anxiety & Panic Disorders — SSRIs, SNRIs, therapy techniques, and holistic approaches
  • Depression — Antidepressants, psychotherapy, and treatment-resistant options
  • PTSD & Trauma — CPT, trauma-focused CBT, and medication support
  • Bipolar Disorder — Mood stabilizers, atypical antipsychotics, and lifestyle management
  • OCD — SSRIs, ERP-supportive medication, and comprehensive care
  • Insomnia & Sleep Disorders — Sleep hygiene, CBT-I support, and medication when needed
  • Alcohol Use Disorder — Medication-assisted treatment and recovery support
  • Pharmacogenomic Testing — DNA-guided medication selection
