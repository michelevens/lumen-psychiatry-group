### 1.1 Provider Photo (Week 1)

### 1.2 Google Business Profile (Week 1)
- Create/optimize Google Business Profile for "EnnHealth Psychiatry"
- Add photos, services, hours, insurance list, appointment link

## Phase 3: Conversion (Weeks 4-8) — Turn Visitors into Patients

### 3.1 Insurance Verification Tool (Stedi API)
- Real-time "Check My Insurance" on site
- Patient enters Member ID + DOB → instant copay/deductible estimate
- Removes biggest barrier to booking ("Will my insurance cover this?")
- Requires: Stedi API key + NPI number

### 3.2 SMS/Chat Widget
- Add SMS opt-in for appointment reminders
- Consider Tidio or similar free chat widget for quick questions
- Impact: Captures visitors who aren't ready to book but want to ask

### 3.3 Referral Program
- "Refer a friend, get $25 off your next visit"
- Simple referral code system via Google Apps Script
- Impact: Word-of-mouth is #1 driver for mental health providers

### 3.4 Email Nurture Sequence
- New patient inquiry → 3-email welcome sequence
- Email 1: "What to expect" + booking link
- Email 2: "Meet Dr. Michel" + testimonials
- Email 3: "Insurance & pricing made simple" + DPC info

## Competitive Advantages to Protect

1. **Continuity of care** — Same provider every visit (big platforms rotate)
2. **5-star reviews** — Every single review mentions personal attention
3. **DPC pricing** — Flat-rate, no surprise bills, cancel anytime
4. **B2B services** — Facility + ER revenue stream competitors don't have
5. **Speed** — Small practice can ship features in days, not quarters
